About Us

The orchestration platform where humans and AI agents work together.

Thinkroid builds AI harnesses and interfaces that make AI accessible to everyone. We believe anyone — regardless of background, age, or technical skill — should be able to work with AI agents and benefit from the AI era.

Our flagship project, Thinkroid Space, uses a gamified pixel-art interface to turn complex multi-agent orchestration into something intuitive. No coding required — if you can play a game, you can manage an AI team.

We're a small team based in Vancouver, Canada, building at the intersection of humans and artificial intelligence.
